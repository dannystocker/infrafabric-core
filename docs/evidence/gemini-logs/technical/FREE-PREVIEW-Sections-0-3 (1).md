Different AI communities optimize for different objectivesâ€”research, trading, healthcare, defenseâ€”while sharing protocols for identity, routing, and audit. **Cooperation through incentives, not control.** Air traffic control doesn't force planes to flyâ€”it makes coordination profitable. **Every architectural choice in InfraFabric flows from these principles:** Reciprocity scoring rewards cooperation. Pluralistic clusters enable diverse objectives.
