# IF.guard — Council Review Checklist

- Quorum ≥ 15 / 20; approval > 50%; supermajority advice ≥ 80%.
- Contrarian veto enabled at ≥ 95% approval (cooling‑off 14 days).
- Evidence: observable artifacts, toolchain validation, uncertainty bands.
- Dissent captured and published; follow‑up tasks assigned and dated.
