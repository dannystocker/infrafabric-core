## Appendix: Scoring Methodology

### Priority Score Components (1-100 scale)

**Influence Level (30 points)**
- Direct budget authority: 25-30 pts
- Requirement setting: 20-25 pts  
- Portfolio/ecosystem influence: 15-20 pts
- Thought leadership: 10-15 pts

**Responsiveness Likelihood (25 points)**
- Fresh mandate (2024-2025 appointment): +10 pts
- Recent relevant activity: +10 pts
- Stated relevant problem: +5 pts

**Strategic Fit (25 points)**
- Perfect InfraFabric use case: 20-25 pts
- Strong alignment: 15-20 pts
- Good fit with adaptation: 10-15 pts

**Timing (20 points)**
- Active buying cycle now: 15-20 pts
- Planning phase (6-12 months): 10-15 pts
- Future opportunity (12+ months): 5-10 pts

### Tier Assignment Logic

**Tier A Criteria** (must meet 2 of 3):
1. Direct budget/procurement authority
2. Technical decision-making power for infrastructure
3. Active buying cycle or deployment in progress

**Tier B Criteria**:
- Influential thought leader, standard-setter, or ecosystem builder
- Can accelerate adoption through amplification but not direct buyer
- Policy/requirements influence that shapes market
